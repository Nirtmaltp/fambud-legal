3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the FamBud service
  • Authenticate your identity when you log in
  • Display your financial data (transactions, budgets, analytics)
  • Enable household and group features (shared budgets, bill splitting)
  • Send push notifications for scheduled reminders (if enabled)

4. Third-Party Services

These services process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies. Data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure (AWS, region: EU or US depending on project configuration).

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (via Settings → Delete Account), all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers, including:

  • Your profile and authentication credentials
  • All transactions you created
  • All group memberships and split data
  • All debts and IOUs
  • Household data (if you are the last remaining member)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data (edit your profile in-app)
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data (in-app or by contacting us)
  • Data portability — export your data (via the Backup feature in Settings)
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nirmalthomas123@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit)
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data
  • JWT-based authentication tokens with automatic expiration
